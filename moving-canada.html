Moving Safety Tips

Please keep these tips in mind when booking a moving service:

  • Do not pay the full amount upfront. Pay the balance upon successful delivery.
  • Confirm the driver's vehicle license plate matches their profile/description when they arrive.
  • Agree clearly on the hourly rate and estimated time before the move begins.
  • Do not transport prohibited, illegal, or hazardous materials.

Eri Shipping Hub connects you with independent drivers. We do not handle payments and are not liable for lost or damaged goods. Please verify the driver independently.
